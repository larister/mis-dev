24 hour Competition

As well as films from world-class talent, we will be putting on a competition for filmmakers in Lewes. This is a great opportunity to hone your creativity and show the world what you can do. Further details below:

  • Brighton Film School will offer a £100 prize to the winning team of this challenge
  • Registration of two person crews and payment of £10 entrance fee (£5 for students) should be done in advance. Please register before the 5th of October online at the bottom of this page.
  • Production meeting at 8pm on Friday the 6th of October in Lewes, with the competition commencing immediately after the meeting. The venue is the Studio in The Depot. The production meeting will include briefing of the project and we will issue an information pack.
  • At the end of the competition, films should be uploaded via We Transfer to the festival email link by 8pm on Saturday the 7th of October
  • The selected films will be screened on Sunday the 15th October with two free tickets for each selected crew
  • Crews are suggested to use the Straight 8 app, as well as any camera of choice and edited using whatever editing software available to the filmmaker.
  • The film may be either a drama, a documentary or experimental
Do's and Don'ts
  • No use of profanities (swearing), use of imitation weapons, copyright music, photos, or film/TV clips
  • Running length not to exceed 3 minutes/180 seconds including titles and credits
  • Crews are responsible for their own safety and that of the public
  • The film must be delivered in H264 format
  • Sound may be recorded in camera or by using an external mic; alternatively it can be added in post-production.
